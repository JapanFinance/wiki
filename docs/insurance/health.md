### Shakai hoken

The vast majority of employees will be enrolled by their employer in shakai hoken for their health insurance and pension. Premiums for shakai hoken are based on your withheld employment income.

## Private insurance

Since public insurance and monthly maximum-out-of-pocket protect from almost all majors costs, private insurance is not generally useful in Japan, and only covers accessories like extra costs for private room or stipend per day in hospital (hospital stays are usually shorts).
